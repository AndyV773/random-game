Love Gambling

Introduction

Love Gambling is based on the classic game "Casino War", it is a game of chance. The goal is to have a higher card than the dealer, which doubles your money. The cards are ranked as they are in poker, with "2" being the lowest and "A" being the highest. For more information, on the classic game check out Casino War.

How to play

  1. Add or Remove Players:

    Use the plus and minus buttons to add or remove players, up to a maximun of 2 players.

  2. Add Funds:

    To play, you must add funds using the Add Funds button. This will add funds in increments of 100, with a maximum of 1000.

  3. Choose Stake Amount:

    Select the amount you would like to stake each round using the stake buttons 5 10 20. The orange button highlights the active amount, this amount will be charged for each player on the board.

  4. Draw Cards:

    Press the BET button to draw a card for each player and the dealer. The higher card wins, and the winner receives double the stake amount on the scoreboard.

  5. Cash Out:

    When you have finished playing, you can cash out your score using the Cash Out! button. This will remove both player's scores from the board and add any remaining funds to the total. You can then compare you total amount to the initial funds to see if you came out on top or not.

Remember, this is a complete game of chance. Good Luck!
